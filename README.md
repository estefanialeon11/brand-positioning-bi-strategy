# Business Intelligence Marketing Strategy – Brand Positioning

## Project Overview
This project focuses on the design and implementation of a digital marketing strategy based on Business Intelligence to improve the positioning of a local lingerie brand in the Ecuadorian market.

The project applies a data-driven approach to identify key marketing challenges, including lack of customer segmentation, limited use of performance metrics, and absence of analytical tools for decision making.

Through a structured strategy, the project integrates data analysis, KPI monitoring and digital marketing planning to enhance brand visibility, engagement and conversion.

---

## Problem Statement
The brand faced several challenges in its digital marketing strategy:
- Lack of customer segmentation
- Limited use of data for decision making
- Low visibility and engagement in digital channels
- Absence of KPI tracking and performance analysis

These issues limited the effectiveness of marketing efforts and impacted the brand’s positioning in the market.

---

## Objective
To design and implement a digital marketing strategy based on Business Intelligence that improves brand positioning, increases audience engagement and supports data-driven decision making.

---

## Methodology
The project follows an action-research approach and is structured in five main phases:

1. Diagnosis and customer segmentation  
2. Content strategy planning  
3. Strategy execution  
4. KPI measurement and monitoring  
5. Data-driven decision making  

Additionally, the project includes continuous training to support the adoption of analytical tools and ensure long-term sustainability.

---

## Business Intelligence Approach
The project integrates Business Intelligence concepts by:
- Defining key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Structuring data for analysis
- Monitoring marketing performance
- Supporting decisions based on data insights

---

## Expected Results
- Increased digital reach and brand visibility  
- Higher engagement with target audience  
- Improved conversion rate from followers to customers  
- Better understanding of customer segments  
- More effective marketing decisions based on data
